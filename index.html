What is game personalization?

Game personalization is something we should all consider when purcahsing a game or trying a game for the first time! What kind of personality do you have? What game is compatible with it?

Why is it important?

Game personalization is essential when it comes to introducing yourself to gaming. What if the first game you try isn't fit to what you're going to like? There are many games in the sea, and there's definetely one that fits your playstyle.
